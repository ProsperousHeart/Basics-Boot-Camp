# What Happens When I Enroll?

This bootcamp is free for you to play with and learn from.

If you would like to get direct feedback as you move through this program, then there are several options.

## Option 1: Bootcamp Barracks

Access to the bootcamp barracks - private community where all questions about the bootcamp are answered. There are no dumb questions, only opportunities to learn.

## Option 2: Cohorts

Cohorts where you will grow with other students and have more direct, hands on support.

## Option 3: Direct 1:1 Support

Direct 1:1 support & live coaching opportunities.
